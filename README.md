# ❄ STILLPOINT

*Between Mind and Matter*

---

STILLPOINT is a contemporary tabletop RPG set in a world that looks almost exactly like ours — people have phones, jobs, and opinions about things. The extraordinary exists alongside the ordinary without replacing it. What most people don't know is that reality is not fixed. It is *agreed upon.*

---

## The World

All things — stone, sky, self — persist through a fragile equilibrium between two primordial forces: **Arcana** (the physical, the *is*) and **Psyche** (the conceptual, the *understood*). Where these forces meet and hold, reality stabilizes. That place of balance is called the Stillpoint.

Humanity lives within a small pocket of that stability: **Lake Nexus** — an oasis of known, agreed-upon reality in a vast and largely unmapped expanse. To most people, Lake Nexus is the entire world. It is not. It is only the part that hasn't come undone yet.

---

## The Three Realities

All existence moves between three states. Life is balance between them. Death is transition through them.

**The Stillpoint** — Reality is agreed upon. Objects have form. Names have meaning. Cause leads to effect. The only place where things remain defined.

**The Cogitarium** — Reality is shaped by thought. Memory becomes structure. Emotion becomes force. Identity becomes law. Real — not metaphor — but governed by mind rather than matter. Where minds go after death, if they let go of their anchor.

**The Void** — Reality is indistinguishable. All possibilities exist simultaneously. No distinctions can hold. Meaning collapses under total overlap. The source of all physical matter, and the destination of all physical matter. Not evil. Not safe.

---

## Three Kinds of Extraordinary Person

Most people live and die within the rules of the Stillpoint. Three categories of person do not.

### Mages
Reality is not fixed — it is *negotiated*. Mages understand that the Stillpoint is built on consensus, and that consensus can be influenced. Through will, knowledge, and **Magic Weight** — the universe's willingness to yield to an individual — they reshape matter, alter cause and effect, and impose new definitions onto reality.

Magic Weight can be inherited through bloodlines, earned through consistent practice, or atrophied through neglect. Low weight can be supplemented by **Magic Restrictions**: philosophical frameworks that substitute earned legitimacy for raw cosmic favor. A mage's restriction framework is a direct expression of their worldview — two mages with identical weight will develop entirely different magical identities based on their philosophy.

The five great systems of restriction are: **Contracts** (magic as agreement), **Alchemy** (magic as transformation), **Language** (magic as word — to name something correctly is to have power over it), **Movements** (magic as physical action), and **Forging** (magic as making — the most accessible system, and the most legacy-oriented).

To most of humanity, mages don't exist. They operate through hidden networks and layered realities, living parallel to ordinary life. Their primary collective agreement is secrecy.

### Psions
The mind is not contained within the body. Psions are born with — or forced into awakening — a **Mind's Eye**: a metaphysical organ that enables direct perception of and interaction with Psionic Energy, the mental substance produced by every living mind.

Psionic energy is visible to an open Mind's Eye as *smoke* surrounding a person. The smoke's character communicates everything about its source — a wisp from an ordinary person, a river from an untrained Psion who cannot conceal their presence, controlled stillness from a master. A void-exposed individual produces no smoke at all. Only silence where a mind should be.

The psionic training progression is also a moral one. **Close** — learning to silence the Mind's Eye voluntarily. **Focus** — selective perception, directing awareness rather than receiving everything. **Reach** — active extension, the first point at which a Psion becomes actively dangerous. **Beyond** — specialization: telepathy, mind reading, soul interaction, hypnosis. Each step is a deeper and more ethically significant reach into another mind.

An untrained Psion does not experience their ability as power. They experience it as *noise* — constant, unfiltered, involuntary. Finding a teacher is not education. It is survival.

The general public has no knowledge that Psions exist. Governments do.

### Metas
Some people don't break reality. They are born outside its expectations. Metas are individuals whose relationship with physical reality sits at the far outer edge of what reality already permits — no cosmic negotiation, no metaphysical organ, no system or tradition. Just an unusual configuration of existing rules.

The man with the strength of ten men is not defying physics. He's at an extreme point of what physical reality already allows. Unlike Mages and Psions, Metas are *public knowledge* — they have legal frameworks, public discourse, and varying degrees of social acceptance. No unified category of ability exists. Every Meta is case by case.

## The Void

Beneath all reality lies the Void — called by some *Mayhem*. It is not nothing. It is everything, without separation. Infinite, undifferentiated potential — the source from which all physical things emerge, and where all physical things eventually return.

The Void is not a threat. It is simply natural. What it is not is *safe for the mind.*

The Void's nature — dissolving distinctions, blending all definitions into one — is fundamentally incompatible with what a mind *is*. A mind runs on distinction: identity, memory, meaning, the separation of self from other. Prolonged exposure strips the higher faculties. The abstract grows quiet. Philosophy, identity, relationships, meaning — all dimming. What the body demands grows louder. The self erodes downward until only the most primal layer remains.

The most insidious part: *the person rarely notices.* The very faculty being consumed is the one that would allow them to recognize the loss.

Void exposure in living individuals stems from **chaos casting** — working magic without restrictions. This is why Magic Restrictions are not merely mechanical. They are protection. A restriction is a contract with reality that keeps a mage anchored to the Stillpoint rather than pulling directly from the undifferentiated dark beneath it.

Recovery is possible. It requires stopping the chaos casting, and surrounding oneself with the things that maintain definition — people who knew them, places that carry their history, contexts that insist their identity is real. The self cannot reconstruct itself in isolation. It needs other things to reflect it back until it remembers what it was.

---

## Souls

A soul is not a separate metaphysical category. It is the *mind externalized upon death*. When the body ceases to function, the mind — which produced psi throughout life — becomes untethered. It persists as an externalized presence, no longer anchored to physical form.

Souls are visible to any open Mind's Eye as a baseline ability — no training required. Interacting with them meaningfully requires Reach and beyond. Magic can affect the physical world and the body. It cannot touch what departs after death. Souls are exclusively psionic territory.

What becomes of a soul afterward is a matter of nature and tether. Some remain as spirits — anchored by identity, unfinished things, or the simple weight of who they were. Others transition into the Cogitarium, joining a shared existence of thought beyond the physical entirely.

---

## Tone

STILLPOINT is contemporary. The magic doesn't have to be present in every scene to be present in the world. A scene can be about breakfast and still be set in a world with pocket dimensions.

**Magic is a weapon. Psi is a muscle.** Magic is something you *wield* — external, visible, loud. Using it announces itself. Psi is something you *develop* — internal, invisible, quiet. Using it is indistinguishable from nothing happening, to anyone without a Mind's Eye.

When someone does magic, there is no ambiguity. Reality bends visibly. When a Psion works, nobody knows. An object moves and there is simply no explanation. A person's feelings shift and they chalk it up to their own mood. Psi does not ask permission to already be in the room.
